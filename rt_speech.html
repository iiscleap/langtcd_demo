Language Familiarity for Talker Change Detection

Step 1: Choose any Language. This creates the playlist for the test. There are 110 English samples and 106 Chinese samples. You can change the language anytime during the test.
Step 2: Press "Play". This will play the audio. Be attentive.
Step 3: Press 1 or 2. Press the number of talkers you detected in the audio, as soon as it stops playing. Your response time will be computed.
Step 4: Press "Next" and go to Step 2.. This will go to the next audio sample. You can relax when needed.
